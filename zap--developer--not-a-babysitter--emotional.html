  1. A session stops for a nod. It lands in your inbox, flagged and waiting.

  3. Make it yours. Right-click the ⚡ button and type the exact phrase to fire. Multiline is fine; it sends verbatim.

Needs actual words? Alt+1/2/3 fire AI-drafted replies and Alt+S opens your saved snippets, same toolbar. The Zap key itself is re-mappable in Settings → Keyboard.
