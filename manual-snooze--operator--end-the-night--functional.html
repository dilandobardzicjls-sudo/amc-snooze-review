Closing the laptop shouldn't mean losing your place

You stay because the thread lives on the screen. Walk away and tomorrow starts with twenty minutes of finding it again.

The return does the remembering. Every snoozed session comes back at your time and reopens right where it stopped. Until you answer, the ask waits.

Try it. One snooze for the lot

  1. Shift-click the sessions that can wait, then right-click and pick Snooze. One snooze parks the lot. Solo works too: press H with a session active.
  2. Pick when they come back, or type it in your words. The palette offers 2 Hours From Now plus the three natural times coming up next. Typing works too: "fri 9am", "in 2 hours", even "tomorow 5pm" with the typo. AMC shows what it understood before you press Enter.
  3. That's it. They're off your screen until your time. Then they return together. A message wakes any of them early, and the amber badge changes or ends a snooze.

Every session comes back to its own chat, marker at the spot where you stopped.
