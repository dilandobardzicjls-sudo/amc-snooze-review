Try it. It's seconds

  1. Right-click the session and pick Snooze. Or press H with the session active. Seconds either way.
  2. Pick when it comes back, or type it in your words. The palette offers 2 Hours From Now plus the three natural times coming up next. Typing works too: "fri 9am", "in 2 hours", even "tomorow 5pm" with the typo. AMC shows what it understood before you press Enter.
  3. That's it. The session is off your screen until your time. Then it returns on its own. A message wakes it early, and the amber badge changes or ends the snooze.

It runs on your computer, so leave the machine on. You're the one going to bed.
