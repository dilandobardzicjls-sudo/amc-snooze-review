You know where your attention should go

Snooze is how you act on it. Park the asks that can wait and give each one a return time. Nothing sneaks back early. Even a question that lands mid-snooze waits quietly for its turn.

And you set that time in your own words. Type it like you'd say it: "fri 9am", "in 2 hours", even "tomorow 5pm" with the typo. AMC shows what it understood before you commit.

Try it. Two clicks

  1. Right-click the session and pick Snooze. Or press H with the session active. Two clicks either way.
  2. Pick a return time, or type one in your words. The palette offers 2 Hours From Now plus the three natural times coming up next. Typing works too: "fri 9am", "in 2 hours", even "tomorow 5pm" with the typo. AMC shows what it understood before you press Enter.
  3. Done. It leaves the sidebar and inbox until your time. Then it returns on its own. A message wakes it early, and the amber badge edits or ends the snooze.
