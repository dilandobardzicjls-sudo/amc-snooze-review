Try it. Save one reply

  1. Open the Quick Replies project in your sidebar → the Quick replies tab.
  2. Click “+ Add reply,” paste your phrase, and switch on Auto-send when selected.
  3. Mid-chat, press Alt+S and pick it, or just hit its number key, and it sends in under a second.

Prefer to check before it sends? Leave Auto-send off and the reply drops in for you to edit. Sort replies into folders, or have an agent add them for you.
