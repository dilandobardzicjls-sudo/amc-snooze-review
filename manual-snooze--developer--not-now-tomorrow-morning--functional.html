Try it. Type a time

  1. Right-click the session and pick Snooze. Or press H with it active.
  2. Type when it should come back, in plain words. "tomorrow morning", "in 3 hours", "fri 9am", even "tomorow 5pm" with the typo. AMC reads back what it understood before you press Enter.
  3. It leaves your sidebar until your time, then returns on its own. A message wakes it early.

The return time is stored, so it survives a restart or quitting AMC. It runs on your machine, so leave that on.
