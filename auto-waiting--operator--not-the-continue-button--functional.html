But what if it’s waiting on me?

One rule decides: who is it waiting on? A question, an approval, anything meant for you pings you instantly, always. Only waits on the agent’s own work get held.

And a held wait doesn’t sit forever. Anything still silent after 30 minutes pings you anyway, with the reason attached. Your number, anywhere from 5 minutes to 4 hours.

Auto-waiting never changes what the agent is allowed to do, only when you get pinged. Your approval and permission settings are untouched. Nothing is hidden: every held ping stays visible, tagged, on the record.
