Try it. Two clicks

  1. Right-click the session and pick Snooze. Or press H with the session active. Two clicks either way.
  2. Pick a return time, or type one in your words. The palette offers 2 Hours From Now plus the three natural times coming up next. Typing works too: "in 2 hours", "530p", even "tomorow 5pm" with the typo. AMC shows what it understood before you press Enter.
  3. Done. It leaves the sidebar and inbox until your time. Then it returns on its own. A message wakes it early, and the amber badge edits or ends the snooze.
