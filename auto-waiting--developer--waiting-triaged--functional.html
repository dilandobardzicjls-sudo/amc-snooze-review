Your knobs.

  1. Hover ⏱ for the matched rule, on demand.
  2. Window: Settings → Sessions → Waiting period, 5-240 min.
  3. Per session: Check in pokes now, Keep waiting extends the hold.
  4. Auto check-in (opt-in, off by default): continues a merely-waiting agent, max 3×.
  5. Deliberate deferral: snoozeSession takes "friday 9am" in the palette; the session keeps running while hidden (unlike Pause, nothing freezes).
  6. Escape hatch: AMC_DISABLE_WAITING_DETECTOR=1.
